Required Documents

Please prepare the following documents to open your account

Identification Card or Passport

A valid national ID or passport is required for identity verification and compliance with regulatory requirements.

4x6 Photo

Recent photograph for identification purposes. Please ensure the photo is clear and taken within the last 6 months.

Canadia Bank Account

Required for financial transactions and linking with trading systems. This ensures secure and efficient fund transfers.

Investor Identification Number

Apply for an Investor ID number directly to SECC or through Golden Fortune Securities. We can assist you with this process.
