Our Milestones

Key moments that shaped our journey and Cambodia's securities market

2010

SECC Licensing

In November 2010, SECC granted licenses to fifteen securities firms, including Golden Fortune, setting the foundation for Cambodia's securities market infrastructure.

2011

Official Launch

Golden Fortune officially inaugurated operations in December 2011, becoming one of the first securities dealers ready to support Cambodia's emerging capital market.

2012

CSX Trading Begins

Cambodia Securities Exchange began operations with the first IPO from Phnom Penh Water Supply Authority, marking the beginning of active securities trading in Cambodia.

2015

Market Growth

As Cambodia's securities market matured, we expanded our services to include comprehensive investment advisory and wealth management solutions for growing client base.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched advanced digital trading platforms and mobile applications, making securities trading more accessible to retail investors across Cambodia.

2025

Leading Forward

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services, maintaining our position as a trusted partner in Cambodia's evolving financial landscape.
