Important Information

Trading Days

Trading occurs Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays. Weekend and holiday trading is not available on CSX.

Auction Methods

CSX uses single-price auctions for opening and closing sessions, and multi-price auctions for continuous trading.

Session Duration

Total trading time is 7 hours daily, with the longest session being continuous trading from 9:00 AM to 2:50 PM.
